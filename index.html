The Mechanic

Train With Your Gaze

SPANTHER uses real eye-tracking technology to turn your attentional capacity into a game mechanic — then progressively trains it to be stronger.

01 ///

LOOK & AIM

Your gaze is your targeting system. Direct your eyes at incoming asteroids to lock on. The eye-tracking SDK maps precisely where your attention lands.

02 ///

HOLD & CHARGE

Sustained focus is your power source. Hold your gaze steady on a target to charge your weapon. Larger asteroids demand deeper, longer concentration.

03 ///

BLINK & RELOAD

A deliberate blink reloads your weapon charges. The game rewards conscious blinking — the natural eye refresh that most screen addicts have forgotten.
